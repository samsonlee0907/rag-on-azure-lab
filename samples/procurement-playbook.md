# Procurement Playbook

## Intake
The requester must submit a business justification, supplier details, and expected contract value.

## Contract Review
Legal review is required for net-new suppliers, data-processing addenda, or any contract term longer than 24 months.

## Approval Matrix
Contracts below $50,000 require director approval.
Contracts between $50,000 and $250,000 require finance and procurement approval.
Contracts above $250,000 require vice president approval and risk review.
