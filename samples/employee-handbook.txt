Employee Handbook

Travel Policy
Employees may book economy travel for trips shorter than six hours.
Vice presidents may approve business-class travel when a customer workshop requires overnight recovery time.

Expense Policy
Receipts are required for any reimbursement above 25 USD.
Managers must approve expenses within five business days.

Security Policy
Customer data must not be stored on unmanaged devices.
Incidents must be reported to the security response mailbox within one hour.
